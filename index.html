Frequently Asked Questions

The fine print, simplified.

Clear answers to common questions about MechServi operations, partners, and protection policies.

How does doorstep vehicle service work?

Once you book, a verified MechServi partner is dispatched to your location (home, office, or breakdown site). They bring all necessary eco-friendly cleaning agents, diagnostic tools, and equipment. The service is completed right in your parking slot or driveway without requiring you to supervise. You receive real-time before/after photos and approve the job directly in the app before the partner is paid.

Are the service partners certified and background-verified?

Yes. Every partner goes through a rigorous onboarding process that includes identity verification (Aadhaar & PAN cards), professional reference checks, mandatory police background checks, and an in-person skill assessment test. Additionally, partners undergo specialized customer service and tool handling training before accepting bookings.

What happens if my car or bike requires additional parts?

If the technician detects that a part needs replacement, they will inspect it and upload photos of the worn component. They will raise an additional work request within the app detailing the cost of the original spare part. Work will only proceed once you review the quote and tap "Approve Work". This guarantees there are zero surprise charges on your final invoice.

What is waterless car washing, and is it safe for my vehicle?

Waterless car washing uses premium, highly-lubricated polymers sprayed directly onto the vehicle. These polymers encapsulate dirt particles, lifting them away from the paint. The technician then wipes them off gently with high-grade microfiber towels. It is 100% safe, scratch-free, gives a deep wax shine, and saves over 150 liters of water per wash.

What does the emergency roadside assistance cover?

Our emergency assistance runs 24/7. It covers flat tyre puncture repairs, battery jumpstarts (using portable booster packs), fuel delivery (up to 5 liters of petrol/diesel), keys locked inside retrieval, and towing services to the nearest major service station if the vehicle cannot be repaired on the spot.

Is my vehicle insured during the service?

Yes. In the rare event of accidental damage during the cleaning or repair process, MechServi provides platform liability insurance coverage of up to ₹25,000 for bikes and up to ₹1,00,000 for cars, ensuring total peace of mind for our customers.
